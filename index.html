Hello!

I am Ena Robinson (She/Her) -
A final year commerce undergrad at SRCC.

Growing up in Udupi, a coastal town in Karnataka, I have spent a lot of time close to nature. Early on I have taken a keen interest in diverse fields, not just academics, and find my peace in playing sports, crafting and engaging in banters across philosophy, the startup ecosystem, and climate change.
Stepping into college I got involved in Enactus, an international student-run organization building sustainable social entrepreneurship projects, and worked on 4 projects in the water, craft, and waste management sectors, some of which have been acclaimed at the national and international level for their potential to improve lives.
Given my experience interning at development consulting firms and early stage startups, I have the ability to set the vision and translate those into a strong set of strategic solutions. After more than two years of rewarding conversations with hundreds of people and trying to understand their problems, I have realised my passion for building products that people love. This is why I believe in the positive power of businesses in creating opportunities and transforming lives.
